Welcome to Taniti
Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. With sandy beaches, rocky coastlines, a safe harbor, lush rainforests, and an active volcano, our 500 square mile paradise offers endless exploration and relaxation opportunities for visitors. Discover our rich culture, indigenous population of 20,000, and the perfect blend of tradition and modern hospitality.
From exploring our rainforests to visiting our active volcano, Taniti offers adventures for everyone. Enjoy chartered fishing tours, snorkeling, zip-lining, helicopter rides, and more. Visit our local history museum, microbrewery, or dance club. With new attractions being added regularly, including a nine-hole golf course opening next year, there's always something new to experience.
Whether you're looking for luxury resorts or budget-friendly options, Taniti has accommodations for everyone. Choose from our four-star resort, family-owned hotels, growing number of bed and breakfasts, beach houses, or private rentals. All lodging is regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government to ensure quality and safety for our guests.

Getting Around Taniti
Taniti is easy to explore with a variety of transportation options. Most visitors arrive by air at the small international airport, but some also come by small cruise ships docking in Yellow Leaf Bay. Once on the island, you’ll find public buses, taxis, and rental cars available to help you get around. Many visitors also enjoy walking or biking around Taniti City.
